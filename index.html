How it works

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Verify is built for agency principals, not compliance officers. If you can send an email, you can use it.

1
Identify the customer

Determine which client or counterparty requires an AML check before proceeding with the transaction.

2
Send a branded verification link

Generate a link directly from Verify - branded with your agency's logo - and send it to your client. No paperwork, no back and forth.

3
Receive the results

Get the completed AML report back automatically. Everything is logged with a full audit trail, ready if AUSTRAC ever asks.

4
Request additional checks if needed

If further verification is required, simply resend another link. Each check is tracked and recorded independently.

Pay only for what you use
